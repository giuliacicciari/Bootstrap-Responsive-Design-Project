About Rate My Snacks

Welcome to Rate My Snacks, where every bite gets its moment to shine! I’m on a mission to try new snacks from around the world and give honest, no-nonsense ratings on taste, texture, packaging, and overall snackability. Whether it’s the latest chip flavor, a nostalgic childhood treat, or a random find from the international aisle, nothing is off limits. Explore my reviews, share your own snack opinions, and join the community of fellow snack enthusiasts who believe that good snacks deserve good attention.

This Week's Snacks

Wonderful Pistachios-Dill Pickle 2/10

These were so gross, I do not recommend. So salty, felt like I needed to chug water right after eating one singular nut.

Everything But The Bagel Nut Duo 6/10

Honestly super yummy. They were a bit too powerful in flavor but I would definitely purchase again. Makes a good pair with a sandwich.

Pip's Fries 9/10

So yummy. These were the best snack purchase I have made in so long. Very savory and very crunchy.

Think Protein Bar: Thin Mint 5/10

So good, tasted just like a thin mint. However, the texture was a bit odd. A little dry and left me thirsty.

True Fruit Milk Chocolate Strawberries: 2/10

These are so overhyped. They felt watery and the chocolate flavor was dull. The strawberries were very sour.

Toffee Crunch Oreos: 1/10

I only have one word... disgusting. These were way too sweet and the taste was so unappetizing.
